Geopolitics

At REVA

The recorded narratives showcase that politico-diplomatic, strategic, military, economic, technological rivalries among States result in sub-regional, regional and global wars that are fought on land, at sea and in the air in nearly every part of the world. The advent and use of nuclear weapons, militarization of the space and the cyber domain has added newer complexities to wars. By all accounts, the two World Wars, the Cold War and the ongoing conflicts between Russia and Ukraine and the rivalry between the US and China are deeply immersed in geopolitics with global ramifications.

VISION

The REVA Center of Excellence for Geopolitics and International Studies is a dynamic Center situated at REVAUniversity committed to exploring and advancing India's pivotal role in the complex global landscape of the 21st century. Through thought-provoking discussions and exchanging ideas, the Center serves as a platform for igniting intellectual curiosity and fostering a comprehensive understanding of geopolitics and international studies. By facilitating dialogue among scholars, professionals, decision-makers, and emerging leaders, the Center envisions fostering intellectual curiosity, contributing to India’s growth and global peace and stability.

By nurturing a community of scholars and practitioners committed to intellectual rigour and independence, the Center intends to contribute to global peace and stability while advancing India's interests and values. Through its multifaceted approach, the Center seeks to make a meaningful impact on shaping India's role in the evolving dynamics of the 21st-century global system.

MISSION

At the heart of the Center's mission is the aspiration to inspire and equip the next generation of thinkers and policymakers to contribute meaningfully to India's emergence as a responsible and influential global force. Through various initiatives, the Center promotes engagement and interaction across diverse disciplines and perspectives, encouraging dialogue and collaboration among stakeholders. By fostering multidisciplinary research and encouraging holistic comprehension, the Center aims to enrich the discourse on India's foreign policy choices and geopolitical strategies, ultimately contributing to the nation's growth and development on the world stage.
